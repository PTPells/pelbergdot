Chalk

Your chat room, with super powers.

Team: Caret Labs
Platforms: iOS | web
Time: Winter 2015 - Spring 2017

Context

Chalk was our exploration into "unlocking" viral growth. We intended to preserve Jot's, core "spontaneous group chat" interaction, while remixing the surrounding mechanics to more clearly demonstrate to people why the service, and their experience with it, would be markedly better the more they brought their existing friends onto it.

Outcome

The changes we made were effective at encouraging the kind of growth we sought. Chalk's 1 person, 1 chat room paradigm appealed to people with existing audiences. Specifically, teenage Instagram influencers overwhelmed by DM requests from their followers to talk, but who lacked a space to do so at scale.

In subsequent months, this niche of teen influencers helped drive Chalk to grow into a top 100 app in the U.S. App Store (Social Networking).

Chalk's growth, took us further down the "live streaming" path wherein we discovered the "live selling" behavior that was emerging on Persicope and Facebook Live. The behavior that would become the basis for Baskets.
