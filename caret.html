Caret Labs

A social software studio

Team: Jeffrey Noh + me
Platforms: iOS | Android | web
Time: Spring 2014 - Summer 2018

Caret Labs was the venture-backed startup Jeff and I founded in 2014. In our four years working as Caret Labs, we designed, built and grew three products, each the outgrowth of the previous:

Jot inspired a vibrant community to emerge atop it and attracted venture capital investment.

Chalk scaled to support 100,000+ daily interactions and became one of the top 100 Social Networking apps in the U.S. App Store.

Baskets helped small businesses using Facebook Live as a sales channel multiply their revenue by automating how they tracked sales.
