Caret Labs

Caret Labs was the venture-backed startup Jeff and I founded in 2014. In our four years working as Caret Labs, we wholly designed, built and grew three products: Jot, Chalk and Baskets.

Jot inspired a vibrant community to emerge atop it and attracted venture capital investment in the process. Chalk scaled to support hundreds of thousands of daily interactions and became one of the top 100 Social Networking apps in the U.S. App Store. Baskets helped small businesses use Facebook Live as a sales channel and generate an additional $400,000+ in revenue.
