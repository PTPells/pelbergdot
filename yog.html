Yog

Run virtually with anyone in the world in real time.

By 2011, running with a phone was familiar. A range of apps offered runners the ability, to record and subsequently, analyze and socialize through the data they generated from their activities. This was great, assuming you'd already developed a running habit.

However, for people who hadn't started running, but wanted to, having someone to run *with* was most effective at getting them there.

We designed Yog for this purpose. Yog matched runners from 103 countries and created an experience that simulated the running together in the same place.


Core Components

  • Real-time audio and visual cues
  • Limited post-run data
  • Pre-scheduled runs


Outcome

Yog grew to become a top 25 app in the U.S. App Store (Health and Fitness) and at its peak, supported 25,000+ MAU in 103 countries.
